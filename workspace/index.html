OUR MISSION

Seahawk technologies is a unique innovation that incorporates risk into supply management. External news and data relevant to the user's business are compiled into a database. Sentiment analysis is then used to assign a connotation to this acquired information, in order to evaluate the potential effect of this information on the user's supply chain. Seahawk condenses and organizes this infomation before notifying the user, allowing for an organized, timely presentation of facts. This technology allows the user to make proactive decisions regarding their supply chain, before potential distruptions have the opportunity to take effect. Things happen. We're here to let you know when they do.
