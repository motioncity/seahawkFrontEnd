OUR FOUNDERS

CEO & PRESIDENT :

Michael Loveless

Michael Loveless is the acting President and CEO of Seahawk Technologies. Michael is well versed in information technologies, with over 18 years of SAP experience in business intelligence and analytics. With degrees from Cornell, the University of Illinois-Chicago, and Penn State, Michael has accumulated SAP experience through work in both the US and Europe. Michael has worked as the Managing Director of Sapiex Associates- a SAP consulting services firm- since 2004.

VP PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT :

Vamsi Godavarthi

Vamsi Godavarthi is the Vice President of Product Development of Seahawk Technologies. To this role, Vamsi brings a Masters degree in Information Technology from NJIT, as well as over 20 years of experience in product development. Vamsi has worked in product development, ERP solutions, and business intelligence across a diversity of fields including pharmaceuticals, consumer, and retail industries. Vamsi has also consulted about SAP projects with several Fortune 100 companies as both a Business Intelligence Architect and Implementation Partner.

VP SUPPLY CHAIN INNOVATION :

Peter Scilla

Pete Scilla is the Vice President of Supply Chain Innovation of Seahawk Technologies. Pete has acquired over 40 years of Operations, Supply Chain, and IT experience through his role as Vice President of Global Supply Chain for many pharmaceutical companies, along with a 20 year career with Johnson and Johnson, where he held leadership positions in departments of Supply Chain, Planning, Business Intelligence, IT, and New Product Launch. Pete holds certifications in Operations and Supply Chain as well as in Information Management.

VP CLOUD SOLUTIONS & STRATEGIES :

Dieter Hotz

Dieter Hotz is the Vice President of Cloud Solutions and Strategies of Seahawk Technologies. With 29 years of SAP experience, Dieter has been involved in over 200 SAP implementations at several Fortune 500 companies. Deiter is the CEO and founder of Delta Consulting Inc., and is the Co-Founder and Vice President of Technology at Integration Associates, both a national services partner to SAP America. Dieter also has experience in software development with SAP America and SAP Germany. Hotz holds an MBA from the University of Karlsruhe in Germany specializing in Computer Science and Business Administration.
